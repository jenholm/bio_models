# REFERENCE_CLAIM_MATRIX.md

Matrix mapping each manuscript claim to its supporting citation(s) and assessing citation strength.

## Citation Strength Definitions

- **Strong**: Foundational empirical or theoretical work in the field; widely cited; directly supports the claim
- **Moderate**: Relevant empirical work; supports the claim but with some distance
- **Supporting**: Tangentially relevant; provides context but not direct evidence
- **Weak**: Limited relevance; citation may be stretched
